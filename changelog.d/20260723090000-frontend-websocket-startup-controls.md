Frontend-model WebSocket connect, connection, and channel APIs now accept `timeoutMs` and `signal` startup controls while preserving their synchronous SnapReq handle contracts. Configured and per-call controls compose without extending the configured budget, caller abort reasons are preserved, and controls stay out of wire payloads. Session cancellation also stops managed retry timers, reconnect and buffered-event flush work deterministically; explicit disconnect disposes the owned client so reconnect remains cancellable.
