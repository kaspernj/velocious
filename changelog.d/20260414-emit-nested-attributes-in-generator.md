- Generated frontend-model `resourceConfig()` now emits `nestedAttributes` from the resource's `static nestedAttributes` declaration. The client `save()` walker reads this to decide which dirty children to ship in the payload — without it the walker has nothing to act on and nested writes silently no-op. Function-valued options (e.g. `rejectIf`) are not serialized into the generated frontend model — they remain server-only policy.
