`background-jobs-main` now uses event-driven dispatch by default: it drains on enqueue, on worker readiness, on Beacon broadcasts to a dedicated dispatch channel, and at the exact `scheduled_at_ms` of the next future-scheduled job via `setTimeout`, instead of running a fixed-interval `SELECT * FROM background_jobs WHERE status='queued' …` poll every second. Transient drain errors auto-retry. Add `backgroundJobs.dispatchStrategy: "polling"` (or env `VELOCIOUS_BACKGROUND_JOBS_DISPATCH_STRATEGY=polling`) to restore the legacy behavior.
