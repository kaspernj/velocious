Set descriptive `process.title` on every background-jobs (and server/beacon) process so `ps`/`top`/`htop` identify them instead of showing a wall of generic "node" entries — essential for diagnosing which processes and jobs consume CPU/RAM. The main, worker, server, and beacon processes are named; forked/spawned job runners are named after the job they run. A job class may declare `static processTitle = "..."` for a custom, human-readable title (otherwise the runner falls back to `velocious job-runner: <JobName>`); velocious sets it for the duration of the job and restores the runner's base title when the job finishes.
