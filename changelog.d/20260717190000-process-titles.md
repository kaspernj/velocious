Set descriptive `process.title` on every background-jobs (and server/beacon) process so `ps`/`top`/`htop` identify them instead of showing a wall of generic "node" entries. Forked and spawned job runners are named after the job they are executing (`velocious job-runner: <JobName>`), so operators can see at a glance which jobs are running, how many forks each consumes, and which are eating CPU/RAM — essential for diagnosing worker resource usage. The main, worker, server, and beacon processes are named too.
