Add per-queue dispatch priorities for background jobs (`backgroundJobs.queues[name].priority`, default `0`). The main process dispatches higher-priority queues before lower-priority ones regardless of enqueue order, so a small time-critical queue can't be starved by a flood of low-priority work sharing a worker pool. Priority composes with the per-queue concurrency caps — a higher-priority queue at its cap is skipped so dispatch falls through to the next eligible lower-priority job — and jobs within a priority keep FIFO order. Negative priorities sink a queue below the default; future-scheduled jobs stay strictly time-ordered.
