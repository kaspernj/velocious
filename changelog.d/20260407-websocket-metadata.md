WebSocket connections now support client-to-server metadata. Clients can call `setMetadata(key, value)` to attach key-value pairs that are sent to the server on connect and whenever updated. Server-side `WebsocketSession` exposes `getMetadata()` and notifies active channels via `onMetadataChanged(metadata)`. `FrontendModelBase.setWebsocketMetadata(key, value)` delegates to the internal WebSocket client.
